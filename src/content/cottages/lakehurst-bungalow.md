## Welcome to Lakehurst Bungalow

Escape to the serene beauty of Lake Erie at the Lakehurst Bungalow. This charming 4-bedroom retreat offers stunning water views, modern amenities, and the perfect blend of comfort and tranquility. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway or a family vacation, this 1,700 sq ft bungalow provides the ideal setting.

The Lakehurst Bungalow features a thoughtfully designed layout that maximizes the spectacular lake views. With 4 comfortable bedrooms and 2+ bathrooms, there's plenty of space for up to 10 guests to relax and recharge.

**Bedrooms & Bathrooms:**
- 4 beautifully appointed bedrooms
- 2+ full bathrooms
- Sleeps up to 10 guests comfortably
- Quality linens and comfortable mattresses

**Living Spaces:**
- Bright, airy living room with lake views
- Modern entertainment setup
- Cozy seating areas
- Large windows bringing in natural light

**Kitchen & Dining:**
- Fully equipped modern kitchen
- All necessary cookware and appliances
- Dining area with seating for 8-10
- Coffee maker and kitchen essentials

One of the bungalow's highlights is the outdoor space. Enjoy your morning coffee or evening cocktails on the private balcony or deck while taking in the breathtaking Lake Erie views. Access the shared private beach (exclusive to the property owner and 2 other properties) via a gravel walkway down to the boardwalk with umbrellas.

**Outdoor Features:**
- Private balcony with lake views
- Spacious outdoor deck
- Shared fire pit area
- Gas grill for BBQs
- Gravel walkway down to boardwalk with umbrellas
- Green space and lawn area
- Shared private beach access (between property owner and 2 other properties)
- Outdoor seating
