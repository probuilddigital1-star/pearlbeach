## Welcome to Pearl Beach Lakehouse

Discover your perfect Lake Erie getaway at our stunning 5-bedroom lakefront retreat. With breathtaking sunset views, direct beach access, and 2,400 square feet of comfortable living space, the Pearl Beach Lakehouse offers an unforgettable vacation experience for families and groups of up to 13 guests.

### The Space

Our spacious lakehouse features an open-concept main floor with panoramic lake views. The fully equipped kitchen flows seamlessly into the dining area and living room, creating the perfect space for gathering and creating memories with loved ones.

**Bedrooms & Sleeping Arrangements:**
- 5 well-appointed bedrooms
- 3 full bathrooms
- Comfortable sleeping for 13 guests
- High-quality linens and bedding provided

**Living Areas:**
- Large open living room with lake views
- Cozy seating for the whole group
- Smart TVs with streaming capabilities
- Board games and entertainment

**Kitchen & Dining:**
- Fully equipped modern kitchen
- All cookware, dishes, and utensils
- Coffee maker and essential appliances
- Dining table seats 12+
- Additional seating at kitchen island

### Outdoor Paradise

Step outside to your private lakefront oasis. The expansive patio area is perfect for morning coffee, afternoon BBQs, and evening gatherings around the fire pit. Watch the spectacular Lake Erie sunsets paint the sky in brilliant colors - a nightly show you'll never forget.

**Outdoor Amenities:**
- Private beach access
- Lakefront patio with seating
- Fire pit area
- Gas grill for BBQs
- Lawn games
- Direct water access

### Location & Activities

Located in beautiful Saybrook Township in Ashtabula County, Ohio, you're perfectly positioned to explore Lake Erie's wine country and natural attractions.

**Nearby Attractions:**
- Grand River Valley wineries - 10 minutes
- Covered Bridge Wine Trail
- Geneva State Park - 12 minutes
- Beach activities and water sports
- Local restaurants and shops

### House Rules & Check-in

- Check-in: 4:00 PM
- Check-out: 10:00 AM
- No smoking inside
- Pets considered (please inquire)
- Quiet hours: 10 PM - 8 AM
- Maximum occupancy: 13 guests
